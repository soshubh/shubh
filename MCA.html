What's MCA?


MCA course is a Computer Science PG Degree whose curriculum is primarily based on application software development in diverse areas. However, the Computer Science Major program also focuses on providing the students with a thorough and sound background in theoretical and application-oriented courses relevant to the latest computer software development.
MCA duration ranges from three to two-year course that prepares a candidate to become a quality software professional with the advanced and updated knowledge base, which is necessary to meet the needs of software industries. The MCA course program focuses on providing a sound theoretical background as well as good practical exposure for students in the relevant areas.
The MCA course syllabus is intended to provide modern, industry-oriented education in applied computer science. It aims at producing trained professionals who can successfully meet the demands of the information technology industry.
As the IT and the software industry are dynamic and fast-growing, all the programs are designed to keep in view the industry's requirements. Also aims at the understanding of the fundamentals of computing among the students so that they can compete in the present-day global situation.
